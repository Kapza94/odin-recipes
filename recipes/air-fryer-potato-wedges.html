Air Fryer Potato Wedges

Deliciously crispy air-fried potato wedges are perfectly seasoned and ready to serve in just over 30 minutes. It doesn't get
any easier than this! Serve with ketchup for a tasty appetizer, or toss with some grated Parmesan for a cheesy potato side

How to Make Potato Wedges

You'll find the full recipe below, but here's a brief overview of what you can expect:

Seasoned potato wedges are placed in the basket of an air fryer and cooked for 10 minutes. The wedges are
with tongs and cooked for an additional five minutes, to ensure maximum crispiness.

This recipe doesn't require pre-soaking the potato wedges before air frying. However, if you have time to
soak the potatoes in cold water for about 30 minutes before seasoning and "frying," the recipe might yield
even crispier results.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1.5 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground paprika
  • ½ teaspoon parsley flakes
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1-8th teaspoon ground black pepper
  1. Step 1

    Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    Cut each potato in half lengthwise. Cut each half in half lengthwise, and then cut each quarter in half lengthwise. You will have 16 wedges.

  3. Step 3

    Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Add olive oil, paprika, parsley, chili, salt, and pepper; mix until well combined.

  4. Step 4

    Place 1/2 of the potato wedges in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    Flip wedges with tongs and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Remove to a plate.

  6. Step 6

    Repeat to cook the remaining wedges.
